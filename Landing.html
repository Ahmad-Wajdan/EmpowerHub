Mukhyamantri - Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana

The "Mukhyamantri - Majhi Ladki Bahin Yojana" is a Maharashtra government initiative. It's launched by the Women and Child Development Department. The scheme empowers women aged 21-65. It focuses on economic freedom, health, and nutrition. It also aims to strengthen women's family roles. Beneficiaries receive â‚¹1,500. The payment is made through Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT). The goal is to provide financial assistance. By providing regular financial assistance, the scheme contributes to improved social security for women across Maharashtra.

MeitY Digital India Internship Scheme

MeitY's "Digital India Internship Scheme" offers students practical learning. It applies classroom knowledge in real-world scenarios. Preference is given to those with relevant experience and strong academics. The scheme provides 24 internship slots. These slots cover 18 distinct areas. The internships are located in New Delhi. The goal is active student participation. It is an opportunity to gain experience in the digital sector. Students with higher qualifications may be prioritized. It is organized by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.

Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyaan

The PMGDISHA scheme aims to boost digital literacy in rural India. It's an initiative by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY). The goal is to digitally educate six crore rural individuals. This targets roughly 40% of rural households. It focuses on covering one member per eligible household. The scheme operates across all States and Union Territories. It improves digital access in rural communities. Individuals without smartphone access are a primary target. Young adults who have discontinued their college education are a key group.
